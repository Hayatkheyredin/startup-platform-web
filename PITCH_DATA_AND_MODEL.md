### TAM / SAM / SOM (one slide)

| Layer | Definition | Number (order of magnitude) |
|-------|------------|-----------------------------|
| **TAM** (Total Addressable Market) | Total SME financing gap in Ethiopia + economic loss from gender gaps that could be partly captured by a platform like ours | **$6B+** (financing gap) and **$3.7B/year** (gender gap cost)—we address a portion of this |
| **SAM** (Serviceable Addressable Market) | Women-led and skill-based ideas + existing small businesses seeking scaling capital in Ethiopia (our segments) | **~33,000–55,000** women-led / skill-based MSEs (if 30–50% of 111K+ MSEs) + new idea submissions; in value terms, a **meaningful share of the $6B gap** (e.g. 10–20% = **$600M–$1.2B** addressable) |
| **SOM** (Serviceable Obtainable Market) | What we can realistically serve in the next 3–5 years (first cities, first cohorts, first deals) | **1–5% of SAM** in year 5: e.g. **hundreds to low thousands** of founders, **tens of millions USD** in facilitated investments, **premium + commission + equity** revenue |

## 2. Business model — B2B / B2C / C2C

**Headline:** *Multi-sided platform: B2B + B2C + C2C*

| Type | Who we serve | What we do | How we earn |
|------|----------------|------------|-------------|
| **B2C** (Business to Consumer) | **Women founders** (idea-stage and existing small businesses) | They register, submit ideas, get validated, get partner matching and financial literacy | Equity share when startups generate revenue; optional listing/fee for premium features later |
| **B2B** (Business to Business) | **Investors** (individuals, angels, funds) | They get anonymous deal flow, legal agreement through us, intro to founder after commitment; premium = access to scaling businesses | **Commission %** on investment; **Premium subscription** for scaling-business deal flow |
| **C2C** (Consumer to Consumer) | **Founder–founder** | We match women with complementary partners (e.g. cook + caterer, stylist + hair washer) so they can start or scale together | Strengthens pipeline and success rate; can be bundled in B2C or premium |

**One-sentence:**  
*“We are a **multi-sided platform**: **B2C** for women founders (we take equity when they earn); **B2B** for investors (commission + premium subscription); and we enable **C2C** partner matching so founders find business partners.”*
